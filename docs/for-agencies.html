For agencies

Deliver work that plugs into your client's AI stack

Agencies create the decision work of marketing — brand systems, personas, journeys, briefs, campaign strategy. OSMM lets you deliver it as structured, AI-ready objects your clients' teams and agents can actually use — not another PDF that stalls in a shared drive.

Where great work goes to die

The craft was never the problem. The format was.

Your best thinking ships as a deck, a Figma file, or a Miro board. It looks great in the readout — and then it stalls. The client can't operationalize a sixty-slide strategy. Your personas never reach their CDP. Your journey map doesn't drive their automation.

Six months later the work is half-remembered, and the next agency rebuilds it from scratch. Unstructured deliverables can't be reused, can't be measured against, and can't feed the AI workflows your clients are now standing up.

What OSMM means for agencies

Deliver the decision, not just the document

OSMM is an open standard for the canonical objects behind marketing's decision work. For an agency, it means delivering in two layers: the persuasive artifact you already produce, and the structured object underneath it.

Hand over a Brand Context object, a set of Persona objects, a Journey object — typed, machine-readable, and ready to drop into the client's stack and agents. Same craft, far more usable output.

Your deliverables, as objects

A direct mapping from what you already make

The work you hand over today maps cleanly onto OSMM objects:

Brand book / guidelines → Brand Context object
Segmentation & personas → Audience + Persona objects
Customer journey maps → Journey object
Creative brief → Creative Brief, composed from the objects above
Campaign / activation plan → Campaign Strategy object
Content / editorial plan → Content Strategy object
Measurement & KPI plan → Measurement Framework object

Open builder tools convert your existing decks, docs, and Miro boards into these objects — so adoption changes how you hand work off, not how you do it.

This doesn't commoditize you

The thinking is still yours

OSMM structures the output of your thinking, not the thinking itself. The insight, the big idea, the art direction, the strategic judgment — still unmistakably yours. The standard just makes sure that value survives the handoff and compounds for the client, instead of evaporating in a slide.
